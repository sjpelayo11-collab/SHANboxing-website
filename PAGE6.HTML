    • cross arm left and right 8sec (per side)

    • sumo stance for hamstrings 10secs
    • sumo stance while pushing your body left 2sec, right 2sec then repeat 10x
    • Leg swings (front-to-back and side-to-side – 15 each leg)
    • Lunges with torso twist (10 each side)
    • High knees / butt kicks (20 sec each)
    • Standing side stretch (reach over head, 20 sec each side)
    • Cobra stretch (lying face down, push up chest, 20 sec)
    • Seated twist (20 sec each side)
    • Hamstring stretch (seated reach for toes, 20–30 sec)
    • Quadriceps stretch (standing, pull foot to glute, 20 sec each leg)
    • Hip flexor lunge stretch (20 sec each side)
    • Calf stretch (against wall, 20 sec each leg)

    • ✅ Tip

    • Do dynamic stretches before boxing to warm up.
    • Do static stretches after boxing to recover and increase flexibility.
